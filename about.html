ABOUT BRAND RATE

Creator pricing, made clearer.

Brand Rate is a creator pricing utility designed to help influencers and digital creators estimate a practical starting range for sponsorship conversations.

What Brand Rate does

The free calculator considers platform, audience market, niche, audience size, average reach, engagement and deliverable type.

Why we built it

Creator pricing is often opaque. Two creators with the same follower count can produce very different campaign value, and commercial terms such as usage rights or exclusivity can materially change a deal. Brand Rate turns those inputs into a clear, directional pricing framework.

Free calculator

A quick directional sponsorship estimate with no account required.

Creator Pricing Kit

A paid personalised digital kit with rate guidance, package ideas and negotiation language.

Important note

Brand Rate provides informational estimates and negotiation guidance. It does not guarantee that a brand will accept a quoted fee, and it is not legal, financial or professional representation.
